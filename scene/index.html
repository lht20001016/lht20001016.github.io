Let The Bullets Fly

The objective of this game is to avoid the incoming bullets at all costs. Move with your mouse. You have some abilities at your disposal to better dodge the bullets. Flash, represented in game by an yellow icon and activated by the F key, using flash will instantly teleport your character to the position of your mouse. Ghost is activated by the G key and represented by a blue icon, which grants your character a temporary speed boost. Barrier is activated by the B key and represented by an orange icon. Uisng barrier will grant your character immunity for a brief period of time. Best of luck!
